Rebuild this project **from scratch** as a polished, single-page **interactive presentation website** for **AIGYPT ├Ч AINA**, entirely in **Bahasa Indonesia**. Audience: **khalayak umum** тАФ a live crowd of up to 10,000 people of all backgrounds (students, community elders, business owners), most of whom know nothing about AI. The site is structured as a **30-minute stage talk divided into 4 chapters ("Bab")**, works like a keyboard-driven slide deck with step-by-step reveals, and doubles as a normal responsive website afterward.

The writing must be warm, simple, and story-driven. Zero tech jargon without explanation. Every slide has one idea only.

## Design rules (strict тАФ the previous build failed by over-decorating)
1. **ONE font: "Plus Jakarta Sans"** (Google Fonts; 400, 500, 600, 700, 800). Headlines weight 800, letter-spacing -0.02em. Eyebrows/labels: weight 600, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.18em, 12тАУ13px.
2. **Palette тАФ black with a hint of purple, exactly these tokens:**
   - `--bg:#0B0712` ┬╖ `--surface:#151021` ┬╖ `--border:rgba(167,139,250,.14)`
   - `--accent:#7C3AED` ┬╖ `--accent-soft:#A78BFA` ┬╖ `--text:#F4F1FA` ┬╖ `--muted:#9D93B8`
   - Only gradient allowed: `linear-gradient(120deg,#A78BFA,#7C3AED)` тАФ for one key word per headline, the primary button, and the progress bar. **No cyan, no magenta, no animated background.**
3. One decorative element only: a static radial purple glow behind slide 1 (`radial-gradient(ellipse at 50% 25%, rgba(124,58,237,.22), transparent 60%)`).
4. Max content width 1120px, generous whitespace, all animations тЙд500ms using only transform/opacity, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected.

## Presentation system
- Full-viewport slides (`min-height:100svh`), `scroll-snap-type:y mandatory` on desktop; below 768px snap is off and it behaves as a normal scrolling site (dots hidden).
- **Keyboard:** `Space`/`ArrowDown`/`ArrowRight` тЖТ next fragment, or next slide when fragments are done. `ArrowUp`/`ArrowLeft` тЖТ back. `Home`/`End` тЖТ first/last. `F` fullscreen. `T` presenter timer. `R` replays the AINA chat. Click/tap also advances.
- **Fragments:** marked elements start hidden (`opacity:0; translateY(14px)`) and reveal one per keypress (400ms). On mobile scroll, fragments simply appear.
- **Chapter system:** slides belong to 4 chapters. A small fixed label top-center shows the current chapter (e.g. `BAB 2 ┬╖ AIGYPT`). The right-side slide dots are **grouped by chapter** with a small gap between groups. Each chapter opens with a divider slide (see below).
- **Presenter timer (T):** hidden pill top-left, `mm:ss / 30:00`, starts on first keypress, amber at 25:00, red past 30:00.
- Tiny muted label bottom-left of each slide: `┬▒N MENIT` (values below).
- Thin gradient progress bar top.
